Town Divided Over Court Decisions

By Danna Paola Soriano Gomez | Salem Village

Tension continues to grow in Salem as recent court decisions leave many residents questioning what justice really looks like. While some believe the court is protecting the town from danger, others are starting to worry that innocent people are being caught in the chaos.

Conversations around town have become more cautious, with people choosing their words carefully and avoiding conflict whenever possible. The fear of being accused has created a noticeable shift in how neighbors interact, replacing trust with uncertainty.

As the situation develops, it remains unclear whether the court’s actions will restore order or deepen the division already spreading throughout the community.
